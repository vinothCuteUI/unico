The Unique Plastecowood Recycling Process

How we source our input material

We approach waste management companies who separate and segregate waste. We acquire this waste from them, they then deliver it and we reprocess the waste into super durable SmartawoodTM products.

Our carbon neutral process recycles the plastic

We aim to be as neutral as not only do our recycled SmartawoodTM products have a positive impact on the environment, but our manufacturing process is sustainable too.

Our skilled team transforms the lumber into beautiful products

Waste plastic is extruded into moulds. Here, our skilled team work their magic and create highly durable, hygienic and ecological outdoor products.

Our Product

Sans Bois

Transforming plastic waste into sustainable outdoor wood products, UNICO focuses on recycling, processing, and designing. Our mission is to rescue plastic waste packaging from landfills or incineration, repurposing it into durable, maintenance-free items with a positive environmental impact. Sans Bois, crafted from recycled plastic, stands as our sustainable alternative to wood. Through collaboration with world-class engineers, we replicate wood's elasticity and strength, aiming to decrease wood demand and combat deforestation with this innovative product.

Sustainable Creations

From recycled plastics, we craft study and enduring products such as pen stands, garden bowls, and grow baskets. Engineered for longevity, these items not only offer a rigid structure but also contribute to a longer life cycle. The best part? By choosing our products, you stand against pollution, aligning with our commitment to a cleaner and greener planet.

Sustainable Outdoor Solutions

For enduring outdoor furniture and landscaping, the choice of high-quality structural materials is paramount. That's why we created Sans Bois, a cost-effective timber alternative delivering superior performance and products with infinite longevity. Our recycled plastic lumber doesn't rot, split, or splinter, requiring minimal maintenance. Crafted from 100% recycled household plastic waste, choosing our high-quality material is an investment not only in durable products but also in the well-being of Planet Earth.
